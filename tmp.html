Your questions, answered

What is an invoice?
An invoice is a time-stamped commercial document that records a transaction between a buyer and a seller. If goods or services were purchased on credit, the invoice usually specifies the terms of the deal and provides information on the available methods of payment.
Is this invoice generator really free?
Yes! You can create, download, and send a specific number of invoices for free. We also offer premium plans for advanced features like bulk creation and automated recurring billing.
Can I add my company logo?
Absolutely. Our editor allows you to upload your logo and place it anywhere on the invoice to ensure your documents look professional and branded.
What file formats are supported?
You can download your invoices as high-quality PDFs, which are compatible with virtually all devices and operating systems.
